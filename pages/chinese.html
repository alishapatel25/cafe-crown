Noodles


Noodles are a staple in many cuisines, and there are countless ways to prepare them. Here's a simple recipe for noodles that you can customize to your taste

Ingredients

- 1 cup noodles (rice noodles, wheat noodles, or any other type of noodle you prefer)
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., bean sprouts, carrots, bell peppers)
- 1 cup cooked meat or seafood (e.g., chicken, beef, shrimp)
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Scallions, chopped (optional)

Instructions

1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
3. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic and stir-fry until they are softened.
4. Add the mixed vegetables and cooked meat or seafood, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet or wok, and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes.
6. Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using), and stir-fry for another minute.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Transfer the noodles to a serving platter, and garnish with chopped scallions (if using).
9. Serve hot and enjoy.
